This page demonstrates the different blockquote renderings supported by the theme's
`render-blockquote.html` template: plain blockquotes, blockquotes with caption/cite,
and the various alert/admonition types (`caution`, `important`, `note`, `tip`, `warning`).

Plain blockquote

> The only true wisdom is in knowing you know nothing.

Blockquote with caption and cite

> The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing.
{caption="Albert Einstein" cite="Letter"}

Alert / Admonition examples

> [!tip] Theme toggle
> Use the theme toggle in the header to switch between light and dark modes.

> [!note] Notice
> This is a note without an explicit title; the renderer should fall back to a localized or
> title-cased alert type.

> [!warning] Handle with care
> Warnings indicate potential pitfalls—review carefully before proceeding.

> [!important] Important update
> This step is required for correct operation.

> [!caution] Caution advised
> Pay attention to edge cases when modifying these values.
