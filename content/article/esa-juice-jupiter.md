JUICE is built for endurance, not sprint speed. The trajectory uses gravity assists over years, trading time for fuel efficiency and mission mass flexibility.

Its prime science objective is to characterize icy moons as potential habitats, with special emphasis on Ganymede's subsurface ocean signatures.

For observers, the long cruise phase is a reminder that deep-space missions are logistics marathons before they become science marathons.
