Chandrayaan-3 proved that targeted redesign after failure can produce robust landing performance. ISRO's sequence from Chandrayaan-2 to Chandrayaan-3 is a model of iterative systems engineering.

South polar operations are scientifically valuable because permanently shadowed regions may preserve volatile deposits relevant to future exploration.

The mission also expanded global confidence in a broader set of capable lunar partners.
