Euclid's contribution is not a single image but a statistically powerful survey. Cosmology missions like this depend on stable instrument behavior over long baselines.

Processing pipelines are as critical as hardware. Calibration consistency across years determines whether subtle lensing signatures remain trustworthy.

The mission illustrates how data engineering is now inseparable from space science.
