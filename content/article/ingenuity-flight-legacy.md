Ingenuity began as a technology demonstration and quickly became an operations partner. Flight planning on Mars required hazard mapping, strict battery accounting, and communication choreography with Perseverance.

Its extended campaign changed mission design assumptions for aerial scouting. Future rover missions are now being scoped with airborne assets in mind.

The key lesson is organizational as much as technical: teams that can rapidly integrate new capabilities get compounding returns.
