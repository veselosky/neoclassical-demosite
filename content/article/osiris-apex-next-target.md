After returning Bennu samples, OSIRIS-REx transitioned to OSIRIS-APEX for a new target: Apophis. Reusing spacecraft bus and operations knowledge can stretch mission value at comparatively low incremental cost.

The adaptation still requires fresh hazard analysis and timeline planning because target environments differ.

Mission extensions like this are where institutional memory becomes a scientific asset.
