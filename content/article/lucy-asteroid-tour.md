Lucy will visit multiple Trojan asteroids, effectively sampling a distributed archive of early solar system material. Multi-target missions demand strict navigation discipline and contingency planning.

Each flyby window is short, so observation sequencing must be engineered well in advance with little room for late changes.

If successful, Lucy will provide comparative insights that no single-target mission could deliver.
