Tianwen-1 combined orbiter science and surface operations in a single campaign, requiring careful synchronization between relay assets and rover timelines.

The mission highlighted how integrated architectures can increase science return while introducing mission management complexity.

As Mars mission traffic grows, interoperability and radio spectrum discipline will become more important across agencies.
