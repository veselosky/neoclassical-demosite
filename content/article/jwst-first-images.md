When JWST released its first images, the headline story focused on visual spectacle. The longer story is calibration discipline: teams had to characterize detector behavior, thermal drift, and pointing precision before major survey work could begin.

ESA and CSA contributions remain central to the observatory's global science cadence. Multi-agency scheduling means instrument time is both collaborative and fiercely competitive.

In practice, JWST has become a mission operations case study in international governance and precision engineering at L2.
