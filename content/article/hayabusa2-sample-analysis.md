Hayabusa2's Ryugu samples continue to support studies on carbon-rich materials and early solar system chemistry. The mission's careful contamination controls increase confidence in laboratory interpretation.

JAXA's iterative mission philosophy, from Hayabusa to Hayabusa2 and onward, demonstrates how agencies build capability through disciplined reuse and targeted upgrades.

For planetary science, these samples provide context for models of volatile delivery to early Earth.
